Getting Started
1. Evidence Synthesis

Review published study costs standardised to KES 2027, then click Send to Analysis. The analysis runs automatically and results open in the side drawer.

2. Analysis

Set the effect measure and threshold. Re-run any time after editing strategy data.

Cost-Effectiveness Thresholds (Kenya)
  • 0.5× GDP per capita: KES 154,000 per DALY/QALY
  • SHA Level 3–6: KES 2,240 – 4,480 per day averted
Understanding Results
ICER Table

Incremental cost per additional unit of health outcome versus the reference.

Price Threshold Analysis

Shows the maximum cost at which each strategy would be cost-effective at the chosen threshold (break-even price), how much headroom exists, and the ICER curve as a function of price.

PPP vs Exchange Rate

PPP-adjusted costs (from Synthesis) reflect purchasing power; exchange-rate costs reflect market conversion.
